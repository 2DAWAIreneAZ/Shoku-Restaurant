Contact us

At Shoku, every connection begins with harmony.

We believe that the experience of Japanese haute cuisine starts with a gesture of care — the harmony between guest and host, the anticipation of a refined moment, the quiet promise of our omotenashi.

This is why our contact and reservation service is designed to offer clarity, serenity, and personal attention at every step.

Whether you are planning a dinner, arranging a private event, or seeking guidance from our team.

Reservations

Securing a table at Shoku is more than booking a meal; it is reserving a moment of craftsmanship and Japanese tradition.

Our reservation team ensures each detail — timing, seating preference, allergies, and special requests — is handled with care.

We accept reservations up to 30 days in advance. For large groups or special celebrations, early booking is highly recommended.

To maintain the integrity of the experience, we kindly ask guests to arrive on time.

Our team will be delighted to assist you with any preparation you may need.
